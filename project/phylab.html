PHYSICS LAB




                This lab is very well equipped with magnetic, optical, gravity, waves and sound electrical 
            related equipments.
                There is a wide range of physics laboratory equipments from the common ones like magnets,
            lenses, prisms, glasses, tuning forks etc to sophisticated ones like tangent galvanometer,
            potentiometers, spectrometers, microscopes, dectromagnets etc.
                Walls are decorated with very suitable charts and illustrations.
            
            Objectives
                 ★Measurement of physical quantities with appropriate accuracy.
                 ★Recognition of factors that could affect the reliability of their measurements.
                 ★Manipulations of materials, tools and measuring instruments
