GOVERNMENT HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL

PERIYAKULATHUPPALAYAM

KARUR 639006 

                GHSS, PERIYAKULATHUPALAYAM was established in 1975 and it is managed by the Department of Education. 
            It is located in Urban area. It is located in KARUR block of KARUR district of Tamil Nadu. The school consists
             of Grades from 6 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school
            is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Tamil is the medium of instructions in this school. 
            This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
                
                The school has Government building. It has got 15 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in 
            good condition. It has 5 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master
            and Teacher.The school has Barbed Wire Fencing boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source 
            of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 4 boys toilet and it is functional
            and 4 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 1219 books in 
            its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has 30 computers for 
            teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is not having a computer aided learning lab. 
            The school is Provided and Prepared in School Premises providing mid-day meal.
