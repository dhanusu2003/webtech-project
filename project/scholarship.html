Scholarship Provided By The Government

1 Scholarship to Son and Daughter of Differently Abled Persons, Tamil Nadu ☛ The wards of any differently-abled person holding the Identity card issued by the Welfare Board of Differently Abled can apply for this scholarship.
☛The scholarship is applicable for studies from class 11th to the Postgraduate level (including ITI/Polytechnic diploma courses).		 Financial assistance of up to Rs. 6000/-
2 Scholarship for Differently Abled Students from Class 9th Onwards, Tamil Nadu ☛The scholarship is open to differently-abled students studying in class 9th to postgraduate level (including Vocational, Professional, Medical, and Engineering courses)
☛The applicant should have scored at least 40% marks in the previous qualifying examination.		 Financial assistance of up to Rs. 7000/- per annum
3 Scholarship for Differently Abled Students towards Purchase of Books & Note Books, Tamil Nadu ☛Applicable for Differently-Abled students.
☛ The students studying in classes 1 to 8.		 Scholarship of Rs. 100/- per month (for students of class 1 to 5)and
Scholarship of Rs. 300/- per month (for students of class 6 to 8)
4 R.I.M.C. Dehradun Scholarship, Tamil Nadu ☛Students who enrolled under Rashtriya Indian Military School Dehradun will be able to apply for scholarships. Students will get Rs. 1,000 per month
5 Deceased Government Servants Scholarship, Tamil Nadu ☛The children of deceased employees who died during work will be able to apply for scholarships. Students will get tuition fees, hostel fees and other educational expenses
6 PhD Scholarship, Tamil Nadu ☛The students who are in the first year, second year or third year of their PhD program will be able to apply for the scholarship. Students must be in a government or government-aided college in arts or science courses.
☛Students must score at least 60% marks in their Ph.D. program. 		Students get Rs. 3,000 per month
7 EVR Nagammai Scholarship, Tamil Nadu ☛The scholarship is applicable to the girl students of Tamil Nadu who are pursuing post-graduation from the government college in science and arts. Variable amount
8 Free Education Schemes for BC, MBC & DNC students, Tamil Nadu ☛Students who are under a 3-year degree, diploma, or polytechnic courses are able to enroll under the scholarship.
☛The students family income will not be more than Rs. 50,000/-		 Students will get tuition fees, hostel fees, special fees up to Rs. 10,000
9 Incentive Scheme for Rural MBC/DNC Girl Students, Tamil Nadu ☛The scholarship is not for rural area girl students who are from 3rd to 6th standard and belong to MBC, DNC category.
☛Students family income will not be more than Rs. 25,000\-		 Students from class 3rd to 5th standard will get Rs. 500 and Rs. 1000 to the students of class 6th
10 Thanthai Periyar Memorial Award, Tamil Nadu ☛ The top 2 boys and 2 girls students of every district of Tamil Nadu who score the highest marks in their 10th and pursuing a polytechnic degree will be able to apply for scholarships.
☛ The students must belong to the BC\MBC\DNC category.		 Students get Rs. 3000 for 3 years
11 Perarignar Anna Memorial Award, Tamil Nadu ☛The top 2 boys and 2 girls students of every district of Tamil Nadu who score the highest marks in their 12th and pursuing professional degree courses will be able to apply for scholarships.
☛The students must belong to the BC\MBC\DNC category.		 Students get Rs. 3000 for 4 years
12 National Talent Search Exam NTSE ☛ Students from class 10th standard will be eligible for the scholarship. Students get Rs. 6000 for classes 11th and 12th
